Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

01 — Overview

Our Commitment to Privacy

Pits Stop Collectibles is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a customer. By placing an order or using our website, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

02 — Data We Collect

Information We Collect

When you create an account or place an order, we may collect the following:

  • Full name and username
  • Email address and contact number
  • Shipping address (region, province, city, barangay)
  • Payment reference numbers and transaction proof
  • Order history and wishlist data

03 — How We Use Your Data

Use of Information

Your information is used solely for the purpose of processing and fulfilling your orders. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Process and confirm your orders and pre-orders.
  • Contact you regarding your order status via Facebook or SMS.
  • Coordinate shipping and delivery logistics.
  • Send restock notifications if you have requested them.
  • Maintain your account and order history on our platform.

04 — Data Storage

How Your Data is Stored

Your account and order data is currently stored locally in your browser via localStorage — it is not transmitted to an external server. This means your data stays on your device unless you clear your browser data. We do not use cookies for tracking purposes.

05 — Data Sharing

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is never shared with advertisers or marketing agencies. We only share relevant shipping information (name, address) with our chosen courier partners (LBC, Lalamove) strictly for delivery purposes.

06 — Your Rights

Your Data Rights

As a customer, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data via your Account settings.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Withdraw consent to data processing at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via our Facebook page.

07 — Security

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. However, as our platform currently uses browser-based localStorage for data storage, we recommend not sharing your device or browser with others, and logging out after each session using the Logout option in the menu.

08 — Changes

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

09 — Contact

Questions About Privacy?

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us via our Facebook page or visit our store at Km. 23 Sumulong Highway, Brgy. Sta. Cruz, Antipolo City (10:00 AM – 9:00 PM daily).
