About Us

Pits Stop Collectibles operates as a toy collectibles reselling business which opened its doors to customers in October 2025. The company provides collectors with special diecast car products which include Hot Wheels, Matchbox, and Tomica. The business operates its premises at Km. 23 Sumulong Highway, Brgy. Sta. Cruz, Antipolo City and maintains business hours from 10:00 a.m. to 9:00 p.m.
